Instructions

  • Choose your bylaw first. The bylaw year is defined as the year of expected graduation.
    • For example, students who enrolled in 2014 are expected to graduate in 2019. Their bylaw year is 2019.
  • If your bylaw year does not exist, you can submit it for reviewal using the submit button. It'll be added shortly afterwards.
    • A popup dialog will appear. Choose the bylaw year you wish to add, along with the official bylaw pdf file.
    • The possible bylaw years shown are up to the next 5 academic years only.
    • Only PDF files are allowed.
  • Choose your department from the dropdown menu.
  • Color codes:
    • Passed - Requirements met - Requirements not met
    • Passed (Elective) - Requirements met (Elective) - Requirements not met (Elective)
  • Only select courses which you have already passed. As you select these courses, other courses will be available
  • Humanity courses are present in the bottom as they aren't prerequisites of any courses
  • Deselecting a course will remove all other courses that depends on it
  • Senior project 1 can only be registered after completing 129 credit hours (plus other prerequisites if present)
