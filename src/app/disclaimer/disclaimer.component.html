Welcome to SSP Helper web application!

This app helps you in knowing which courses you can register depending on what courses you have already passed.

Who is this for?
  • Students who failed one or more courses that are prerequisites to other courses.
  • Students who are limited to the number of credit hours they can register and have to sacrifice a course by not registering it at some point.
  • Students who dropped a whole term for some reason.

Basically, if you're behind the proposed study plan of your department, this app is for you. Otherwise, this app isn't beneficial for you

IMPORTANT DISCLAIMER! PLEASE READ!

  1. This app is not affiliated with Alexandria University by any means.
  2. There might be errors with the output of this app. Please revise your options with your advisor prior to actual registration.
  3. This app is made to just give you a vague idea of what courses are possible to register.
  4. The courses listed in this app might or might not actually open during registration. Completing the prerequisites of a certain course doesn't mean you can register it. It might not open for registration afterall
  5. Some courses are more important than others. The output of this app is not a recommendation. It Just shows you the possibilities
